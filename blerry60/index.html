BLErry60 - Wireless 60% PCB

2021-07-27

This is a wireless 60% PCB I made. Featuring the hineybush h60 outline so it can be drop-in for different keyboards.

Description

The entire BLErry series of boards is dedicated to me and my girlfriend's dog Berry.

V1 had MX only. The silkscreen for Berry's art came out pretty bad.

V2 fixes this. I changed from square pixels to round dots for the art.
